Description:

BlissedSkin Nasal Strips provide instant, drug-free relief from snoring and congestion. This 40-count pack gently opens nasal passages to help you breathe easier and sleep better.

Benefits:

  • Instant Relief: Opens nasal passages to reduce snoring and congestion.
  • Drug-Free: Safe solution for colds, allergies, and deviated septums.
  • Comfortable Fit: Hypoallergenic adhesive designed for sensitive skin.
  • Value Pack: Includes 40 nasal strips for a month of restful sleep.

How to use:

  1. Clean Wash and dry your nose to remove any oil (for oily skin, use an astringent)
  2. Place Remove the liner and place the strip on the nose.
  3. Apply Press down ends and rub gently to secure. It is normal to see slight lifting at the edges as it opens your nose.
  4. Remove your nasal strip while washing your face with warm water or when you're in the shower. Always remove the strip gently.
