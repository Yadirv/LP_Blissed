Allergy and Sensitivity Warning

Blissed Skin products contain various active and inactive ingredients that may cause allergic reactions or sensitivities in some individuals. Before using our products: read the complete ingredient list on product packaging and our website, identify potential allergens if you have known allergies or sensitivities, perform a patch test before applying to larger areas (especially face), and discontinue use immediately if you experience any adverse reaction. While we formulate with safety in mind, our products may contain ingredients that some people are sensitive to, including fragrances (natural or synthetic), essential oils, botanical extracts, preservatives, alpha hydroxy acids (AHAs), beta hydroxy acids (BHAs), retinoids or retinol derivatives, niacinamide, and vitamin C derivatives. Stop using the product immediately and consult a healthcare professional if you experience redness, itching, burning sensation, hives, rash, welts, swelling of face, lips, tongue, or throat, difficulty breathing or swallowing, severe or persistent irritation, peeling, blistering, broken skin, or any unusual or concerning symptoms. To perform a patch test: apply a small amount of product to your inner forearm or behind your ear, wait 24-48 hours without washing the area, check for any redness, itching, swelling, or irritation. If no reaction occurs, the product is likely safe for you to use. If a reaction occurs, do not use the product and consult a healthcare professional.
